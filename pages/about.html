Our Story

Taskgram was founded in 2026 with a simple idea — to bridge the gap between businesses that need social media engagement and everyday Nigerians looking for ways to earn online.

We noticed that many Nigerians were struggling to find legitimate ways to earn money online. At the same time, businesses were spending huge amounts of money trying to grow their social media presence.

Taskgram solves both problems — businesses get real engagement from real people, and users earn money for tasks they can complete in minutes from their phones.

Today we are proud to have helped hundreds of Nigerians earn from home and we are just getting started!
